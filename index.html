This is a bingo card for Cadence of Hyrule, based off of Hyphen-ated's isaacBingo.

You can reset and do multiple runs, but in order to check off a square you have to finish that goal during a single run. All runs must be done on the same seed.

In the standard difficulty pattern, the center square is always a challenge or restriction. You may mark the square at the start, but if you ever fail the challenge, you must unmark it.

For goals involving obtaining an item or number of items, you do not need to keep the item(s) after the goal is complete.

Hovering over a goal may provide additional information.

The board is still a work in progress. Feel free to send questions, concerns, and suggestions to RattataJack on Discord.
